The Story

An island of secrets

"Some kindnesses are never truly free."

Set on the mysterious island of Solaris, Siyah-e-Azal is a psychological crime thriller where every secret is connected. As Detective Zeelaf Jeelani hunts a serial killer, a missing chip, and the truth behind a decades-old tragedy, she is forced into a dangerous game with the one man she has spent years trying to destroy—mafia king Zargham Durani. In a world where every alliance is temporary and every answer reveals a bigger lie, one question remains: who has been moving the pieces from the very beginning?

Siyah e Azal follows a tight circle of friends and family as ambition, desire, and buried history collide, drawing them toward a reckoning none of them saw coming.

A note before you read: this novel contains mature and unsettling material, including strong language, substance use, sexual content, and depictions of violence and abuse. It is intended for adult readers who are comfortable with dark, unfiltered storytelling.
